Standard Games

In standard games, a match consists of an odd number of games, such as 1, 3, 5, 7, or 9. To win the match, a player/team must achieve the maximum number of game wins. The player/team who wins more games than their opponent's is declared the winner.

Special Games

In special games, a match consists of any number of games, ranging from 1 to 9. Regardless of wins or losses, both players/teams are required to play all the games in the match. The winner of the match is determined by other criteria or objectives specified within the game rules.
